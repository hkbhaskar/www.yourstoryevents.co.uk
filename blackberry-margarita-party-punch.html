Blackberry Margarita Party Punch!

Published on July 18, 2016 Updated on May 14, 2021 by Eden

  • Share
  • Tweet
  • Pin
  • Email
Jump to Recipe Pin it

This Blackberry Margarita punch is the perfect blend of sour and sweet and serving it over ice dilutes the cocktail making a perfectly refreshing drink!

We’re excited to share this delicious cocktail recipe for a Blackberry Margarita party punch I had at aChambordevent!

It’s a cocktail punch recipe so it serves 8 and it’s SO good! We made our own, easy-to-make sweet and sour mix for it first and then followed the recipe below.

How To Make Blackberry Margarita Party Punch

  • Add all of the ingredients into a pitcher and stir.
  • Serve with a lot of ice to keep it cold and refreshing!

Homemade Sweet and Sour Mix recipe:

  • Add the sugar and water into a medium saucepan and bring to a soft boil to dissolve the sugar. Remove from heat and let cool, then pour into a large container.
  • Add in 2 cups lemon juice and 2 cups lime juice and stir.
  • You wont use all of this for the margarita recipe, so just save the rest for later

How To Serve This Blackberry Margarita Party Punch

You haveto serve this margarita punch on ice because it makes such a difference! The ice dilutes it just a tad and makes it super refreshing!

We served them in my new favorite jars, with lids and a straw. You can find the vintage-style jars on Urban Outfitters. Unfortunately, they come packaged with some random cocktailkit, but I just gave that away so I could enjoy those jars!!

They’re so cute and a great way to serve your margarita! Add some fresh lime slices and blackberries into the jar with ice and you’ve got a pretty charming and tasty Blackberry Margarita cocktail!

Blackberry Margarita – An Easy Punch!

A punch is an easy way to dish out the drinks at parties, it looks great and doesn’t have you being the unpaid mixologist for the night!

The sweet and sour mix really lifts this blackberry margarita punch up a level and it’s simple to make, but the ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a soft boil.

Then, when the sweet and sour mix has cooled just add it to the rest of the ingredients in a punch bowl and voila. A 10-minute punch – easy!

What Does Chambord Taste Like?

One of the key ingredients in this blackberry margarita is Chambord, it works so well in this punch, you’ll be using it in all sorts of cocktails!

Chambord is a French cognac-based black raspberry liqueur, an infusion of the worlds finest raspberries, blackberriesblack raspberries, black currant, Madagascar vanilla and cognac according to its label.

There are other raspberry liqueurs out there, including generics, but Chambord has long been considered the best. Its 33 proof, which is not very strong, and deep purple in color.

Chambord has an intense flavor. Its not quite as sweet as many fruity liqueurs are. The sensation in the mouth is more silky than oily. It feels light rather than heavy. The aftertaste is just raspberry, maybe a hint of vanilla. Delicious!

Top Tips For Blackberry Margarita Party Punch

  • Serve with a lot of ice, to keep it cool and refreshing.
  • Let the sweet and sour mix cool, before mixing into the punch.
  • Keep leftover sweet and sour mix for future use.
  • Use a good quality tequila.

What is the Best Tequila for Margaritas?

If you want to make the very best Margarita, you need the best Tequila and luckily there are some great brands that are affordable too!

Choose Patrn Silver. This Tequila offersnotes of fruit and agavethat complement your Margarita.

You can also choose Herradura Tequila Anejo. It’s a kind of Tequila that boasts of rich citrus flavor with a hint of red pepper. Although, this particular Tequila can be pricy. 

A really great priced Tequila is the Olmeca Altos Plata. It is priced at a very reasonable $25 and it goes well with Margarita mix. Another reasonably priced Tequila is the Casa Noble Aejo, which is priced at $57. 

People often confuse Triple Sec as Tequila, when it is actually orange-flavored liqueur.

BLACKBERRY MARGARITA PARTY PUNCH!

This Blackberry Margarita punch is the perfect blend of sour and sweet and serving it over ice dilutes the cocktail making a perfectly refreshing drink!
No ratings yet
Print Pin Rate
Course: Cocktail
Cuisine: American
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Cook Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 10 minutes
Servings: 8 Servings
Calories: 427kcal
Author: Eden

Ingredients

  • 5 cups of sweet and sour mix, recipe below
  • 1 cup Chambord
  • 1 cup triple sec
  • 3 cups reposado tequila, use a good quality tequila!

Homemade Sweet and Sour Mix recipe:

  • 3 cups water
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups fresh-squeezed lime juice
  • 2 cups fresh-squeezed lemon juice

Instructions

  • Add all of the ingredients into a pitcher and stir.
  • Serve with a lot of ice to keep it cold and refreshing!

Homemade Sweet and Sour Mix recipe:

  • Add the sugar and water into a medium saucepan and bring to a soft boil to dissolve the sugar. Remove from heat and let cool, then pour into a large container.
  • Add in 2 cups lemon juice and 2 cups lime juice and stir.
  • You wont use all of this for the margarita recipe, so just save the rest for later
